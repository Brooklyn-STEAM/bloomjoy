Learn More About BloomJoy!
BloomJoy started with a glue gun, a pile of colorful paper, and a simple idea: feeling good doesn't have to be complicated! Today, BloomJoy is more than a company-it's a reminder. A reminder that joy can be made, one craft at a time.
The founders, Onnjel, Khadidiatou, & Anastasia noticed whenever they felt stressed, they'd end up doodling, decorating old notebooks, or making little handmade gifts for friends. One day, after turning a plain phone case into a bright, flower covered masterpiece, someone asked, "Can you show me how to make one too?" Then someone else asked. And then someone else. Before they knew it, their tiny hobby had turned into something bigger.
They realized something important-people weren't just buying cute crafts. They were smiling more. Laughing more. Taking a break from their busy, overwhelming lives. That's when BloomJoy was born. At bloomJoy, wellness isn't about strict routines or complicated rules. It's about slowing down and creating something with your own hands. Every product is designed to help someone relax and express themselves. The company grew from a small bedroom setup into a bright, welcoming space filled with craft kits, workshops, and endless inspiration. Friends started bringing friends. Families came in together. Even people who said "I'm not creative at all" left with something they were proud of.
